# Polished form controls

Dropdowns, checkboxes, and buttons in the share, copy/move, connection, and team dialogs
now share the app's consistent styling and keyboard focus rings.

## What's new

- **Consistent inputs** — bare `<select>` and checkbox controls across the share dialog,
  copy/move destination picker, connection form, and team invite now match the rest of the
  UI and show a visible focus ring for keyboard users.
